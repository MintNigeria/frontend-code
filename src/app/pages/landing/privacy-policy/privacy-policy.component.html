Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Verification and Authentication of Certificates (“VAC” or “we” or “us” or “our”) solution by the Nigerian Security Printing and Minting Plc (NSPM). CATEGORIES OF DATA

This Privacy Policy delineates the collection, utilization, and safeguarding of information directly or indirectly identifying you ("Data" or "Personal Data"). We limit the acquisition of Personal Data to what is reasonably essential for the intended purposes. Your interaction with us may involve the collection, utilization, storage, disclosure, and transfer of your Personal Data, encompassing details such as name, address, email, telephone number, date of birth, job title, job function, and financial information related to your use of our Website or Application. It may also include student ID/matriculation number, educational documents, professional certifications, and information submitted when you or an organization requests our Services. Additionally, information about your internet connection, IP address, location data, and device details, including password credentials for accessing our Website or Application, may be collected.

COLLECTION OF DATA

VAC Collects Data from Partner Institutions and Credential Providers:
VAC System processes Data from Partner Institutions and Credential Providers, encompassing educational and professional records containing both Directory and Non-Directory Information. These institutions, including universities, polytechnics, colleges of education, professional bodies, and other education agencies, delegate the authority to VAC for the purpose of verifying earned credentials, providing transcript services, and facilitating other VAC-related services.

Associated personally identifiable information collected in the process of using VAC services is only used for the sole purpose for which they are collected. Data retention policies are also applied to deactivated accounts and collected data destroyed after expiration of retention period.

VAC Collects Data from Your Use of Our Services/Website/Application:
We collect Data that is directly obtained from you as you engage with our Services on our Website or Application. We collect information through forms filled out on our Website or Application, as well as documents uploaded in connection with the requested Services.

VAC Collects Data through Automatic Data Collection Technologies:
We utilize automatic data collection technologies, such as cookies, web beacons, and other analytics tools, during your interaction with our Website or use of our Application. These technologies help us gather specific information about your device, browsing activities, and usage patterns:
  • Details of your visits to our Website or use of our Application, including usage data, location data, logs, and other communication data, along with the resources you access on the Website.
  • Information about your computer, mobile device, and internet connection, such as your IP address, operating system, and browser type.

These technologies serve various purposes, including behavioral tracking for online activities, information security to detect and prevent fraud, abusive practices, and security risks. Additionally, they aid in improving our Website and Application, providing a more personalized experience by estimating audience size, understanding usage patterns, storing preferences, recognizing return visits, and expediting searches.

Website Analytics: In specific instances on the VAC Website and Application, we may monitor certain information. Additionally, VAC may track other usage details, such as the type of device utilized to access the Website and Application, and the sections users scroll through, in an aggregated form. It's important to note that personally identifiable information is not collected or utilized by VAC when employing these analytical tools.

Web Beacons: Throughout our Website pages, Application screens, and emails, you may encounter small electronic files known as web beacons (also called clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These elements enable VAC to, for instance, tally users who have visited specific pages, accessed screens, or opened an email, and for other related Website and Application statistics such as recording the popularity of certain Website and Application content and verifying system and server integrity.

Cookies: A cookie is a small file placed on your computer's hard drive, and we only do so with your consent. You can decline browser cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser or mobile operating system. However, opting for this setting might limit access to specific sections of our Website and Application. Unless your browser settings are configured to reject cookies, our system will generate cookies when you access our Website and Application.

We may also collect information you share regarding VAC on social media, such as Facebook, X, Instagram, or LinkedIn.

USE OF DATA

  • Certificate Verification:
    We offer a certificate verification service for partner institutions. This service validates an individual's academic and/or professional certificates/certifications to employers, background screeners, and others requiring confirmation of these data elements for employment or educational purposes, given that the individual is 18 years of age or older. Verification is conducted with the explicit consent of the individual or, if the individual is below 18 years old, with the consent of their parent or guardian. During these verifications, VAC utilizes student Data provided by partner institutions, to whom we are an authorized partner, solely for the purpose of certificate verification.
  • Transcript:
    We only send student transcripts to third parties upon receiving explicit consent from the student. Our secure process facilitates transcript requests, delivering them to designated recipients, including educational institutions or prospective employers, as specified in the order. As part of VAC's electronic transcript delivery methods, we may temporarily retain transcripts until they are accessed by the designated recipient. Any changes in our approach to processing student transcripts will be communicated through notifications on our Website or Application.
  • General Purpose:
    We utilize Data collected from your use of our Website or Application for various purposes, including but not limited to:
    • Presenting our Website or Application and their contents to you and to improve your experience interacting with our Website or Application.
    • Providing requested information and Services.
    • Authenticating submitted information and credentials for verification.
    • Allowing updates, upgrades, or additional copies of existing credential evaluations.
    • Fulfilling any other purposes specified during information provision.
    • Issuing notices about your account, fulfilling contractual obligations, and notifying you about changes.
    • Compiling research information and diagnosing server problems for system efficiency, reliability, and security.
    • Fulfilling orders, sending transcripts, and providing necessary correspondence, including authentication of the identity of service requestors.
    • Informing Partner Institutions or individuals about inquiries regarding education records.
    • Assessing services and operations for improvement, understanding user interests and needs, and conducting research.
    • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements and maintaining accurate records.

RETENTION OF DATA

User Data obtained through our Website and Application will be retained for a period of twelve (12) calendar months and when the time lapses, the data will be expunged, any subsequent requests will be treated as a fresh request, and charges will apply. It will be preserved for the time necessary to provide essential services such as credential verification, facilitation of transcript requests, and the prevention of fraudulent activities. Additionally, the retention period extends to cover the duration required to fulfill the initial purpose of data collection or to comply with legal obligations. This timeframe also encompasses the need to safeguard our legal interests and pursue legal rights. Furthermore, user data will be stored on our backup and disaster recovery systems, adhering strictly to our established policies and procedures. This ensures a comprehensive and secure data management approach in alignment with our backup and disaster recovery protocols. Lastly, for aggregated or anonymized data where individual identification is no longer feasible, retention is indefinite. This approach allows for the continued analysis and utilization of anonymized data while respecting privacy standards and regulatory requirements.

DATA SECURITY

We have implemented comprehensive measures, including physical, organizational, and technological safeguards, to ensure the security of your Personal Data. SSL technology encrypts all connections to our Website and servers through our Application. Sensitive data is encrypted, stored securely, and accessible only to authorized users. Access to our Platforms is restricted, and data is stored in secure servers, physical locations, and cloud infrastructure, including external data centers for enhanced security and availability. Credit card processing is handled by a third-party gateway, and industry-standard access controls and data protection mechanisms secure electronic information. Intrusion prevention and detection systems are deployed to safeguard the integrity of our systems.

The security of your information is a shared responsibility. If you have a username and password for specific parts of our Website or Application, it is crucial to keep this information confidential. We advise using a unique password and refraining from sharing it. For added security, periodic password changes may be prompted. VAC adheres to security best practices by storing a cryptographic hash of your password rather than the original password. Should you forget your password, we can help you reset your account and choose a new password; however, the previous password cannot be recovered.

While we strive to safeguard your personal data, it is important to note that the transmission of information over the Internet is not entirely secure. We cannot assure the absolute security of personal data transmitted to our Website or through our Application. Any such transmission is undertaken at your own risk. We do not take responsibility for the bypassing of privacy settings or security measures within our Application, Website, or servers accessed through our Application.
