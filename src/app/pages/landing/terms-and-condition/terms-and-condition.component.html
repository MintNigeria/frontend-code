DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES:

  • Certificate Verification: We offer a certificate verification service for partner institutions. This service validates an individual's academic and/or professional certificates/certifications to employers, background screeners, and others requiring confirmation of these data elements for employment or educational purposes, given that the individual is 18 years of age or older. Verification is conducted with the explicit consent of the individual or, if the individual is below 18 years old, with the consent of their parent or guardian. During these verifications, VAC utilizes student Data provided by partner institutions, to whom we are an authorized partner, solely for the purpose of certificate verification.
  • Transcript: We only send student transcripts to third parties upon receiving explicit consent from the student. Our secure process facilitates transcript requests, delivering them to designated recipients, including educational institutions or prospective employers, as specified in the order. As part of VAC's electronic transcript delivery methods, we may temporarily retain transcripts until they are accessed by the designated recipient. Any changes in our approach to processing student transcripts will be communicated through notifications on our Website or Application.
