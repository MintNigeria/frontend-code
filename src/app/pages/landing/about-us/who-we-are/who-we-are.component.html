Who we are

Welcome to VAC, the ultimate solution for credential verification.

VAC – Verification and Authentication of Certificates – stands as a groundbreaking digital solution meticulously crafted to revolutionize the certificate verification landscape. This innovative platform, born out of the digital transformation initiatives of the Nigerian Security Printing and Minting (NSPM) Plc, is a testament to our commitment to global best practices and the delivery of cutting-edge security solutions.

Our enhanced web-based verification system employs advanced technology and cutting-edge algorithms, simplifying and automating the certificate verification process while seamlessly managing and facilitating transcript requests.

Built on the foundation of state-of-the-art technology and robust security measures, VAC is designed to establish trust, fortify the integrity of certificates and ensuring the highest standards of security in today's digital world. Our intuitive digital solution guarantees a thorough and efficient authentication process by leveraging a combination of automated checks and procedures, providing real-time verification for academic degrees and professional certifications.

The Verification and Authentication of Certificates (VAC) solution is your go-to comprehensive and reliable platform for verifying certificates. With advanced technology, robust security measures, and user-friendly interfaces, we are dedicated to ensuring accurate and efficient verification processes, building trust, and upholding the integrity of certificates in today's dynamic digital environment. Choose VAC for a seamless and trustworthy credential verification experience.
